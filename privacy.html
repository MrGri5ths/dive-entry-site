Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 April 2026

Dive Entry ("the App") is developed by Colin Griffiths-Williams. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

Dive Entry does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data you enter — including diver profiles, session scores, journal entries, and competition results — is stored locally on your device only.

No Analytics or Tracking

The App does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies. We do not track your usage, location, or behaviour.

Subscriptions

Dive Entry offers optional subscriptions managed entirely through Apple's App Store. Payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Since no data leaves your device, there is nothing to share.

Children's Privacy

Dive Entry is designed to be used by athletes of all ages, including young people. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. All data remains on the device under the control of the device owner.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting the App or clearing its data through your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at griffithscolin@mac.com.
